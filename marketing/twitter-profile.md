# 🐦 Twitter Profile Optimization Guide

**Goal:** Convert profile visitors into discovery calls  
**Target audience:** Bootstrapped founders, non-technical co-founders  
**Tone:** Professional but approachable, founder-to-founder

---

## 📝 Profile Elements

### **1. Profile Picture**

**Options:**

**Option A: Professional Headshot** (Recommended)
- Clean background (white, gray, or branded color)
- Smile, eye contact
- Business casual or casual
- Square crop, 400x400px minimum

**Option B: Casual Working Photo**
- You at laptop/coffee shop
- Shows "working founder" vibe
- Good lighting, professional quality

**❌ Don't use:**
- Logos (you're the brand, not a company)
- Low-quality selfies
- Group photos (which one are you?)
- Cartoons/avatars (trust issue)

## 🎯 Content Strategy

### **Tweet Mix (Weekly):**

| Type | Frequency | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Value tweets** | 3-4x/week | Tips, tutorials, insights |
| **Portfolio tweets** | 1x/week | Showcase your work |
| **Personal tweets** | 1-2x/week | Behind the scenes, journey |
| **Engagement tweets** | Daily | Replies to other founders |
| **Sales tweets** | 1x/week | Direct offer/CTA |

### **Best Times to Tweet:**

**For US audience:**
- 9-11 AM EST (9-11 PM your time)
- 2-4 PM EST (2-4 AM your time)

**For European audience:**
- 8-10 AM GMT (3-5 PM your time)

**Use Buffer or Hypefury to schedule.**

## 📈 Growth Tactics

### **Month 1: Foundation**
- Post daily (even if no engagement)
- Reply to 20 tweets/day from founders
- Follow 100 target customers
- Goal: 200-500 followers

### **Month 2: Momentum**
- Post 1-2 threads/month
- Engage with bigger accounts in niche
- Share client wins (with permission)
- Goal: 500-1000 followers

### **Month 3: Authority**
- Guest on podcasts (promote on Twitter)
- Share case studies
- Build in public (share revenue, clients)
- Goal: 1000+ followers, inbound leads

## 🚨 Common Mistakes

### ❌ **Don't:**
- Buy followers (kills engagement)
- Follow/unfollow (spammy)
- Only post promotional content
- Ignore replies and DMs
- Post inconsistently (1x/week then ghost)
- Use hashtags excessively (#startup #tech #dev)
- Argue with trolls (not worth your time)

### ✅ **Do:**
- Be consistent (daily posting)
- Engage genuinely (not copy-paste replies)
- Provide value first, sell second
- Respond to all DMs (within 24 hours)
- Build relationships, not just followers
- Share your journey (authenticity wins)

## 💬 DM Strategy

### **When someone follows you:**

**Don't:**
- Immediately pitch ("Hey, I see you followed me...")
- Send copy-paste message
- Ask "How can I help you?" (vague)

**Do:**
- Wait 2-3 days
- Check their profile (are they a founder?)
- Send personalized message if relevant

**Template:**
```
Hey [Name]! Thanks for the follow 👋

Checked out [their startup] - love what you're building with 
[specific detail]!

I help founders ship MVPs in 2-4 weeks (senior engineer, 
11+ years). If you ever need technical advice or want to 
bounce ideas around, happy to help.

No pitch, just paying it forward.

Either way, rooting for [Startup Name]! 🚀

Maulana
```
