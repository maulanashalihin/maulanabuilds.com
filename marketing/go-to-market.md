## 🎯 Target Customer Profile

### **Primary: Bootstrapped SaaS Founders**
- **Location:** US, Europe, Australia
- **Stage:** Pre-seed or bootstrap-funded
- **Budget:** $5k-15k available for MVP
- **Pain:** Can't afford agency ($25k-50k), don't trust cheap freelancers
- **Urgency:** Need to validate idea in 30-60 days

### **Secondary: Non-Technical Co-Founders**
- **Background:** Sales, marketing, or domain expert
- **Need:** Technical co-founder alternative
- **Budget:** Has savings or small pre-seed round
- **Pain:** Can't find technical co-founder, agencies too slow

### **Tertiary: Agency Overflow**
- **Who:** Small dev agencies at capacity
- **Need:** White-label development partner
- **Budget:** $5k-10k per project
- **Pain:** Turning away clients due to capacity

## 🎯 Discovery Call Script

### **Opening (2 min)**
```
"Thanks for hopping on! I've checked out [their startup], 
love the idea of [specific feature]. 

Before we dive in, tell me: what's your biggest technical 
challenge right now?"
```

### **Discovery (10 min)**
**Questions to ask:**
1. "What's your timeline? When do you need to launch?"
2. "What's your budget range for this project?"
3. "Have you worked with developers before? What was that like?"
4. "What happens if this MVP succeeds? What's the next step?"
5. "What's the risk if you DON'T build this?"

### **Pitch (5 min)**
```
"Based on what you've shared, here's what I recommend:

[Specific to their needs]

For a project like this, I typically charge:
- Starter ($5k): [scope]
- Growth ($8k): [scope] ← I'd recommend this
- Scale ($12k): [scope]

Timeline: 2-3 weeks
Payment: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery

You get:
- Full code ownership
- Production-ready deployment
- 30-day bug fix warranty
- Weekly progress demos

How does that sound?"
```

### **Objection Handling**

**"Can you do $3,000?"**
```
"I understand budget is a concern. At $3k, I'd have to cut 
[specific features], which would hurt your ability to [their goal].

The $8k package is the minimum for what you need because 
[list specific reasons].

That said, we can phase it:
Phase 1 ($5k): Core features
Phase 2 ($3k): Additional features

Would that work better?"
```

**"I need to think about it"**
```
"Totally understand. What specifically do you need to think through?

[Listen to concern]

Here's what I'd say: 
- I only take 1-2 clients per month
- I have 2 other calls scheduled this week
- If you're serious about this, I'd recommend locking in a slot

Happy to give you 48 hours. Sound fair?"
```

**"What if you don't deliver?"**
```
"Fair concern. Here's my protection for you:

- 50% upfront, 50% ONLY when you're satisfied
- Weekly demos—you see progress in real-time
- Full code handover—you own everything
- 11 years experience—see my portfolio at [links]

I've shipped [X products]. I don't ghost. I don't miss deadlines.

If I don't deliver, you don't pay the final 50%. 
Your risk is protected."
```

## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ **Don't:**
- Compete on price (race to bottom)
- Say "yes" to everything (scope creep)
- Work without contract
- Accept 100% upfront (red flag for client)
- Accept 100% after (you're carrying all risk)
- Skip discovery call (just email quotes)
- Guarantee specific business outcomes

### ✅ **Do:**
- Hold firm on pricing (or offer value-add, not discount)
- Use contract (I can provide template)
- 50/50 payment terms
- Weekly check-ins
- Document everything in writing
- Fire bad clients early (red flags: rude, nitpicky, late payment)

---

## 📞 Red Flags in Clients

**Avoid clients who:**
- Want 100% after delivery (you're a bank, not a partner)
- Want to pay hourly (they'll micromanage)
- Say "this could lead to more work" (empty promise)
- Have had 5+ developers before (they're the problem)
- Want NDA before even talking (amateur)
- Rush decision but then delay payment
- Ask for "small changes" outside scope constantly
